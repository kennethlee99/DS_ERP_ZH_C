## What This Page Is For

Use Planning to place released or planned work into a schedule that supervisors and operators can follow. It is the planner's main view for checking timing, load, and work placement.

## What You See

- A planning workspace with date, machine, group, or view controls.
- Scheduled work shown as rows, cards, or time blocks depending on the selected view.
- Filters for narrowing the schedule to the relevant period or production area.
- Actions for refreshing and reviewing the selected work.

## What You Do

1. Choose the date range and production area for the demo.
2. Find the work order and check where it is scheduled.
3. Adjust only when the visible action and the business scenario allow it.
4. Compare the schedule with Production Orders and Queue System before handover.

## What To Check

- The schedule shows the expected work for the selected period.
- The work appears under the correct machine, group, or planning area.
- The planned sequence makes sense before the supervisor reviews the queue.
- Filters are reset before claiming work is missing.

## Common Issues

| Issue | What it means |
|---|---|
| Planning view is empty | Date, machine group, or page filter may exclude the work. |
| Work appears in the wrong area | The order may need schedule review before release. |
| Queue does not match Planning | Recheck release status and queue filters. |
