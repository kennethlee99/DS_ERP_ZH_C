NCR Non-Conformance

English | 中文

Path: Quality / NCR URL: needs-decision (authenticated screenshot exists, but the exact deployed route was not recorded)

What This Page Is For

Use NCR to record and review non-conforming work, rejected quantities, defect details, review notes, and approval follow-up. Use it when inspection or production review decides that a defect needs a formal quality record.

What You See

  • A list of NCR records with search and filter controls.
  • Header details for the selected non-conformance.
  • Defect and rejection details used during review.
  • Review and approval fields showing who has checked the record.

What You Do

  1. Open the NCR list from the Quality area.
  2. Search for the job, part, NCR number, or date range.
  3. Open the NCR and confirm the defect category, rejected quantity, and description.
  4. Add review notes or approval information when the decision is ready.
  5. Use the NCR reference when communicating with production and quality reviewers.

What To Check

  • The NCR belongs to the correct job, part, and inspection context.
  • The defect description is specific enough for follow-up.
  • Review and approval information is visible before the record is treated as closed.
  • Any on-screen validation message is resolved before saving.

Common Issues
