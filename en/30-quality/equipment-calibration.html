Equipment Calibration

English | 中文

Path: Quality / Equipment Calibration URL: needs-decision (authenticated screenshot exists, but the exact deployed route was not recorded)

What This Page Is For

Use Equipment Calibration to review calibration work for measuring equipment. The page helps quality reviewers decide whether a device can be used for inspection or needs follow-up before production continues.

What You See

  • A calibration record list with search and filters.
  • Equipment identity, calibration dates, certificate information, and remarks.
  • A form/dialog for reviewing or updating a calibration record.
  • Related equipment information used to confirm readiness.

What You Do

  1. Search for the equipment or certificate reference.
  2. Open the calibration record and confirm the calibration date and next due date.
  3. Check whether the certificate or remarks match the physical device.
  4. Escalate overdue, missing, or unclear calibration information before inspection use.

What To Check

  • The equipment name or number matches the device used on the floor.
  • Calibration dates are current enough for the inspection being performed.
  • Certificate information is visible when required by the review.
  • The record is saved before the device is accepted for use.

Common Issues

IssueWhat it means
Equipment is missingThe equipment list may not include the device yet.
Calibration appears overdueThe latest calibration record or next due date may not be updated.
Certificate details are blankThe calibration evidence may not have been recorded yet.
Save failsRequired visible fields may be missing.
